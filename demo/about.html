Meet your teachers

Cara Jo Miller

Cara Jo is a Colorado transplant by way of Detroit, where she helped start the Girl Develop It's Detroit chapter. She coded her first website back in 1996, which was a gallery for her Pokemon drawings. After moving to Detroit, she moved beyond HTML/CSS to PHP and while simultaneous becoming obsessed with WordPress. After five years of urban living in Detroit, Cara Jo decided mountain living was for her. She'd heard the tech and startup scenes in Colorado were great and decided to take the plunge out west. She now wrangles pixels and writes code as a Web Developer and Designer for Rachio. Getting more women involved in tech has always been one of Cara Jo's passions, and that's what she brings to Colorado.

Kathryn Ehlmann

Originally from Central Virginia, Kathryn first became involved with Girl Develop It as a teacher, sharing her passion for Wordpress and front end development. As the Central Virginia chapter grew, so did her passion for helping others empower themselves with new technology skills. Kathryn served as Chapter leader of the Central Virginia chapter before making the jump to the Denver area.

Kathryn has seen firsthand the impact that code has had on many Girl Develop It members' lives. She looks forward to helping many more individuals in Denver and Boulder learn and have fun with technology.

Kathryn is the owner of Wagner Media Works, a web design and development firm that assists organizations of all sizes have an effective and engaging web presence.
